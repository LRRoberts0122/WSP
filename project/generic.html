Who am I??

I'm an artist. Whether it be traditional, digital, or professional, I love every pixel and drop of paint because it creates something unique about what I do. My name is Lindsay Roberts. I was born in Leesburg, FL, and was raised in the general vicinity for my entire life. That's a long time in the sunshine state, which in fact, is more cloudy than sunny.

Since I was a little kid, I've always had a thing for art. Call it talent or a calling, but it's been in my life since I can remember. I even won a regional Fine Arts contest when I was 13.

In January of 2012 I began to pursue my dream of Web Design & Development at Full Sail University. Since then, I've earned several Course Director's awards for my outstanding work.

Now I'd like to do your website and give you a fresh, new look on the web.
