Who am I?

I'm an artist. Whether it be traditional, digital, or professional, I love every pixel and drop of paint because it creates something unique about what I do. My name is Lindsay Roberts. I was born and raised in Leesburg, FL, and have been living here ever since.

I've always had a thing for art. Call it talent or a calling, but it's been in my life since I can remember. That is what brought me to Full Sail, and has helped me realize my dream for Web Design and Development.

Why choose me?

My designs are bold, I have extensive experience in UX design, and I have a process. It starts with a sketch and then I progress to wireframes and Photoshop compositions before building your website. Why do I do this? Because it ensured that your website comes out looking beautiful.

  • Bold Designs: Stand out, be different, and get noticed. I can help you do this.

  • UX Design: Essentially, this is user-focused design. Not only will you be happy, but your clients will be happy, too.

  • Process: The preproduction process is essential. Without it, your website, logo, or graphic may not represent what you stand for.
